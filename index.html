What should you be working on

Build a solution to a local problem using Google Technologies, in accordance with one or more of the United Nations 17 Sustainable Development Goals.

No Poverty

End poverty in all forms, everywhere. This goal aims to eradicate extreme poverty and reduce overall poverty levels worldwide by 2030.

Zero Hunger

The goal is to end hunger, achive food security, improved nutrition among different naations, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030.

Good Health and Wellbeing

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all, at all ages. It recognizes the importance of access to quality healthcare, safe and nutrition food, clean water, and sanitation.

Quality Education

It ensures inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all, recognizes the transformative power of education to break the cycle of poverty.

Gender Equality

Achive gender equality and empower all women and girls. It recognizes that gender inequality is a root cause of poverty and social injustice and that empowering women and girls is essential for sustainable development

Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all human beings, which is a basic human right and essential for human health and environment sustainability.
